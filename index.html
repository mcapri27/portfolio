Website Templates

Old Standard

This is a timeless, elegant website template designed to make your brand shine.

It starts strong with a captivating hero section—your message and a call-to-action button on the left, paired with a striking background image, while a sleek navbar keeps navigation effortless.

The second section highlights your success in a clean 3-cell grid, showcasing your years in business, proud achievements, and certification—all in a refined secondary color for impact.

This design isn't just functional; it tells your story with clarity and confidence, making visitors trust you from the first scroll. Perfect for businesses that value tradition but refuse to be forgotten.

Focal Point

This is a sophisticated website template crafted to command attention and convey credibility.

The design opens with a bold hero section—clean navbar, striking background image, and centered headline with action buttons for immediate engagement.

Below, a structured grid displays your milestones—years in business, clients served, certifications—rendered in a confident primary color for impact.

Every subsequent section features centered text and buttons layered over a subtle, background image

Precise, polished, and purposeful, "Focal Point" ensures your brand stands out with authority—ideal for professionals who demand both elegance and results.

Instant Film

This is the perfect template for storytellers, vloggers, and podcasters who crave nostalgia with a modern twist.

Its hero-section features a bold left-aligned headline, a sleek top-right navbar, and a dynamic background with four "shake-to-develop" photo cards—each with titles and arrow buttons for effortless navigation.

Below, overlapping Polaroid-style containers float over a soft oval blob (in your secondary color), paired with compelling text and action buttons on the adjecent side.

Whether you're a seasoned creator or just dreaming of starting a podcast, "Instant Film" turns your content into a visual journey—inspiring even beginners to hit record and share their voice.

Gallery Neumorphism

This blends soft depth with striking minimalism.

Every element floats effortlessly on its "cream-white" canvas, with cards and buttons that appear gently pressed or lifted through precise shadows.

Hero and About sections transform into immersive bento grids—central titles anchor dynamic background-image cards, while info-rich text balances dark grays and crisp blacks.

Standalone images nest in recessed frames, and text adapts: clean without shadows on solid cards, luminous white glows over imagery.

Designed for intentional storytelling, this template turns galleries into experiences—where every shadow, contrast, and layout choice feels deliberate.

Perfect for creatives who love subtlety with impact.

And it's the template for this website!

Newspaper

Unleash the power of print in the digital age with our 'Newspaper' template—a masterclass in authoritative storytelling!

Designed for bloggers, journalists, and news curators, this template wraps your content in a distinguished newspaper-gray backdrop that radiates trust and timeless appeal.

Every article makes a grand entrance with elegant drop-cap initials, evoking that irresistible old-world editorial charm readers adore.

Each post shines with a dynamic cover photo that pops against the muted tones, paired with bold headlines, prominent bylines (because your voice deserves credit), and clean publish dates for maximum freshness.

Whether you're breaking hard-hitting investigations or sharing thought-provoking commentary, this layout delivers instant credibility and engagement. In a world of flashy, forgettable designs, stand out with sophistication.

Let your content command attention like a front-page headline—because your stories deserve a framework as compelling as your words.

Magazine

Introducing Magazine—a sleek, editorial-inspired website template designed for those with a story worth featuring in TIME, Vogue, or GQ.

The hero section mimics a stunning magazine cover, complete with a high-end photoshoot backdrop, bold typography announcing your name or brand, and eye-catching article teasers.

Strategically placed "Read More" call-to-actions invite engagement, while a subtle barcode in the corner adds an authentic print-inspired touch.

Inside, the layout emulates a premium publication, with full-width article images, elegant headlines, and descriptive captions. The drop-cap styling on paragraphs enhances readability while adding a refined editorial flair.

Perfect for milestone announcements, career highlights, or personal journeys, this template transforms your narrative into a visually compelling, immersive experience—worthy of the world's most prestigious magazines.

Galleria

This is a sleek, immersive template designed to showcase art with elegance and depth.

The fixed left sidebar—minimal yet functional—anchors the experience, while the right side unfolds in a graceful vertical infinity scroll, each card a window into an artist's vision.

The artwork images are draped in a subtle, moody gradient, drawing the eye downward to the details: titles whispered in bold, with price, date, dimensions, and medium tucked beneath in a refined smaller font—like a curator's quiet footnote.

Every card feels like a story half-told, the text left-aligned but rooted to the bottom, as if the art itself is rising from the shadows to meet you.

This is a space where form bows to function, but never at the cost of beauty—where artists are seen, and their work, felt.

Splotcha

"Splotcha" - A Defiant Ode to Digital Chaos.

This audacious template subverts convention with deliberate irreverence. The hero section juxtaposes a frenetic, color-drenched backdrop against a stabilizing gradient—a controlled explosion.

Typography rebels against hierarchy: dominant, ornate fonts clash with subdued fine print, evoking Basquiat's interplay of raw urgency and quiet introspection.

Buttons reject geometry, rendered as organic ink splotches in saturated hues—playful yet purposeful.

The somewhat "disorienting" layout challenges User-Experience norms, demanding engagement rather than passive consumption.

This is anti-minimalism at its most intentional—a template for those who treat the browser as a canvas, not a grid.

Not for corporate conformity; but rather for digital iconoclasts.

Javascript Effects

Haptic Feedback

Ever tapped a button and felt it press down like real life? That's haptic feedback! With subtle scale animations, your website mimics physical clicks, making interactions feel satisfying and responsive. Perfect for boosting engagement—because even tiny details make your brand feel premium.

Fade Scroll

No more boring page loads! Content fades in gracefully as users scroll, making your site feel dynamic yet effortless. It's like a magician revealing secrets—one elegant reveal at a time. Ideal for keeping eyes glued to your message without overwhelming them.

Scroll Assemble

Elements slide in from the sides as you scroll, assembling like puzzle pieces. It's a sleek way to guide attention and add drama to your content. Think of it as a red carpet rollout—each section makes a grand entrance, leaving visitors eager for more.

Photo Grow

Hover over an image, and it smoothly zooms in, drawing focus like a spotlight. It's subtle but powerful—like leaning closer to a painting in a gallery. This effect adds sophistication to portfolios, galleries, or product showcases, making visuals irresistible.

Tool-tip Reveal

Hover over an icon, and a tiny info bubble pops up, explaining features without clutter. It's like having a friendly guide whispering tips in your ear. Perfect for simplifying complex interfaces while keeping your design clean and professional.

Click

A crisp click sound plays with every tap, mirroring real-world buttons. It's the digital “snap” of a light switch—tiny but deeply satisfying. This feedback reassures users their action worked, making your site feel responsive and alive.

Alert

A gentle bell chime rings when alerts appear, like a shop door announcing a customer. It's friendly, not intrusive—perfect for notifications or success messages. Sound design elevates UX, making your site feel intuitive and human-centered.

Conclusion

Each effect is a strategic sprinkle of magic, transforming your site from static to spectacular. Ready to captivate visitors? Let's turn these ideas into your competitive edge.
